Our Story

CEO and Director, Marcus Harris (Right), a 19-year-old Business University student at the time, was astonished to discover a scarcity of lifestyle magazines geared toward young men when he entered his local big-brand retailer on a winters day in 2012. He sought to rectify that. He therefore put in a significant amount of effort while studying for his Business Degree throughout the winter. After battling years of depression, he discovered something he was passionate for. Marcus established a local print magazine called "Generation Z" within the first few months of 2013. He was able to distribute a quarterly magazine to more than 50 stores in Slough and Greater London with the aid of friends. His magazine saw an exponential growth in sales and readership in young adult males. He graduated with his Honours Degree in Business Studies 2015. Hearst Publishing House took notice of his magazine, backed it, and help changed the name to "Vir Vetus" ("Old Man" in Latin, a little tongue-in-cheek change for the Brand) and then worked to turn the magazine into a monthy edition. 

As an editor at Vir Vetus, I can safely say that we have built a reputation from the ground up. Our Magazine is recognised by the Mental Health Foundation and won "Magazine of the Year – Lifestyle" at the ​Newspaper and Magazine Awards 2016. 

We are honoured to partner with companies to support charities and business who help others in need.

Book Your Slot

We know that you, ​the energetic; curious, and ambitious young men of today, are navigating a rapidly changing world, and we want to be your go-to source for the latest trends, tips, and insights on everything from fashion and style, to fitness and technology.

Whether you're looking for inspiration for your next adventure, tips on how to dress for success, or insights on the latest tech and gaming trends, we've got you covered. Our team of expert contributors is here to share their knowledge and experience with you, while also providing a platform for young men like you to share your own stories and perspectives.

Our magazine is designed to be fun, engaging, and relevant to your interests and aspirations. We understand that you are a diverse and multifaceted group, with your own unique passions and goals, and we aim to reflect that diversity in our content.

We also recognize that being a young man today can come with its own set of challenges and pressures. That's why we are committed to promoting positive and healthy approaches to masculinity, and providing a safe and supportive space for our readers to explore their identities and connect with others.
