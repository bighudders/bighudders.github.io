On Remembrance Day we pause to recall,
The heroes who fought and gave their all.
Their bravery and sacrifice we extol,
And honor their memory with heart and soul.

We remember the battles they waged,
And the courage they showed in every age.
The poppy blooms as a symbol of their might,
As we stand in silence and remember their fight.

Their sacrifice echoes through time,
And their legacy continues to shine.
We honor them with every poppy we wear,
As we stand together in gratitude and prayer.

Their memory lives on in every heart,
And we vow to never forget their part.
We will remember them, and all they gave,
On Remembrance Day, and every day.

Feeling hungry and need a delicious breakfast to start your day?

A continental breakfast typically consists of a light and refreshing selection of breakfast foods that are perfect for jumpstarting your morning. It usually includes freshly baked pastries such as croissants, muffins, and danishes, as well as bread, toast, and bagels with a variety of spreads such as butter, jam, and cream cheese.

A continental breakfast is perfect for those who are looking for a light and easy morning meal that will keep them energized throughout the day. It's a great way to start your day on a positive note, and you'll be fueled up to tackle whatever activities or tasks lie ahead.So, why not treat yourself to a delicious continental breakfast tomorrow morning?
